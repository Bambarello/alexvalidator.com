Vision & Contributions

We are a small professional team working on the validator staking services and contributing to the various proof-of-stake projects.

We have been running mainnet staking services for many years, based on the stable and reliable validator nodes in the mainnet: Solana, Oasis, the Graph, Stargaze, Sentinel, Certik, Regen and other networks...

Participated in testnets including, but not limited to: CELO, Nucypher, KEEP, Casper Labs, Pocket, Plasm network, Secret Network, Solana TDS (Active now), Darwinia, Matic, Elrond, Certik, Stafi, Phala, Crust, Sifchain, CODA/MINA, Regen, Crypto.com, BitSong, Sentinel, Compound, Robonomics, Stargaze, eliXXIr, Centrifuge, BitCanna, Axelar, Agoric, Ixo, and others.

  • Solana - early contributors
  • the Graph - early contributor and genesis indexer
  • Oasis - early contributors and genesis validator
  • Sentinel - early contributors and genesis validator
  • Stargaze - early contributors and genesis validator
  • Regen - early contributors and genesis validator
  • Dock - early contributors and genesis validator
